Exhibit A: Breast Cancer Dataset

This evidence file draws on the Breast Cancer dataset curated by Reihaneh Namdari and hosted on Kaggle.
It is a cleaned subset of the US Surveillance, Epidemiology, and End Results (SEER) cancer registry and focuses on women diagnosed with infiltrating duct and lobular carcinoma between 2006 and 2010.

  • Source: SEER population-based cancer registry (US National Cancer Institute), curated for Kaggle.
  • Sample size: 4,024 female patients with breast cancer.
  • Time frame: Diagnoses from 2006‚Äì2010, with follow-up for survival in months.
